# Resolution RES-001 — Initial Board Actions

Title: RES-001 Initial Board Actions

Last-Updated: 2024-12-09  
Alignment: Articles, Bylaws, COI Policy

## Purpose
To adopt governing documents, appoint officers, authorize filings and banking, and establish initial operational parameters for the Corporation.

## Resolutions
1) Adoption of Articles and Bylaws  
   - RESOLVED, that the Board ratifies and adopts the Articles of Incorporation (NC) and the Bylaws as presented, subject to any state filing confirmations and final address/agent details.

2) Adoption of Conflict of Interest Policy  
   - RESOLVED, that the Board adopts the Conflict of Interest Policy and directs annual disclosures and recusal tracking per the policy.

3) Appointment of Officers  
   - RESOLVED, that the following officers are elected to serve until successors are duly elected and qualified:  
     - President/Chair: [TBD Name]  
     - Secretary: [TBD Name]  
     - Treasurer: [TBD Name]  
     - (Optional) Vice Chair or other roles: [TBD Name]

4) Fiscal Year  
   - RESOLVED, that the fiscal year of the Corporation ends on [TBD — e.g., December 31].

5) Registered Agent and Principal Office  
   - RESOLVED, that the Corporation designates [TBD Registered Agent] with registered office at [TBD address, county] in North Carolina, and confirms the principal office address at [TBD].

6) Banking and Authorized Signers  
   - RESOLVED, that the Treasurer is authorized to open and maintain bank accounts in the name of the Corporation; authorized signers shall include [TBD signers]. Dual controls and separation of duties are required for disbursements above thresholds set by the Board.

7) EIN Application  
   - RESOLVED, that the officers are authorized to apply for an Employer Identification Number (EIN) using the information approved by the Board, and the Secretary shall retain the SS-4 workup and confirmation.

8) IRS Exemption Filing (1023-EZ or 1023)  
   - RESOLVED, that the officers are authorized to determine 1023-EZ eligibility using the checklist, and to prepare and submit the appropriate IRS exemption application with required narratives, budgets, and attachments; final submission to be approved by the Board.

9) State Filings and Registrations  
   - RESOLVED, that the officers are authorized to file North Carolina incorporation documents, charitable solicitation registration, and any other required state/local filings, and to maintain renewals per the compliance calendar.

10) Records and Repository Governance  
    - RESOLVED, that the Secretary (or designee) shall maintain official records, minutes, resolutions, and policies in the repository as the system of record, using versioning and PR approvals consistent with the governance constitution.

11) Adoption of Document Retention and Donor Acknowledgment Procedures (When Drafted)  
    - RESOLVED, that upon presentation of the Document Retention Policy and Donor Acknowledgment Procedure, the Board will review and adopt them; interim practices shall follow the governance constitution and applicable law.
